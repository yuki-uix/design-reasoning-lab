Product Overview
Habit Tracker
A calm way for busy working parents to build small daily routines.
The Problem
Most habit apps aren't built for people this busy.
Little time to spare for setup, tracking, or tweaking.
A missed day reads like a red X — a small failure, not a fine one.
They want a few small wins, not another to-do list to manage.
Our Approach
Three principles guide every screen.
01
Small steps
Three to five habits at a time — nothing overwhelming.
02
Gentle nudges
Reminders that show up quietly, at a time that works.
03
No guilt
Missed days stay neutral. Progress is never a grade.
Today View
Three to five habits, one tap to check off.
A short summary shows how the day is going — without turning it into a scoreboard.
Weekly Progress
A simple trail, not a report card.
Filled dots mark a day done. Open dots just mean a day passed — never shown in red.
Creating A Habit
Name it, set a rhythm, pick a reminder.
Three short steps — under a minute from idea to habit.
Our Promise
Consistency that fits into a busy life — without the guilt.
