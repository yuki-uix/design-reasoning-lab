Product design concept

Habit Tracker
for Busy Parents

A calm way to build small daily routines — for parents of kids ages 3 to 12, with five minutes to spare and no time for guilt.

The Problem

Small routines, big friction

Most days already run on a tight schedule before a new habit enters the picture.
Missing one day in most trackers breaks a streak — and the whole habit gets abandoned.
Setup should take seconds, not a settings menu.
“Between school pickup, dinner, and one more email, tracking a new habit is the first thing to slip.”
— a working parent, describing a typical weekday
Design Principles

Calm over guilt

Small on purpose
Three to five habits at a time, never a wall of tasks.
Progress, not perfection
A missed day pauses a streak. It never erases one.
Fits in the cracks
Quick to open between tasks, quicker to close.
