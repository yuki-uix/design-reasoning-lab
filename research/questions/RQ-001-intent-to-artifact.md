# RQ-001: From intent to artifact

## Question

How does an AI design agent transform vague user intent into a design artifact?

## Motivation

Output quality alone does not reveal which assumptions, planning steps, or human inputs shaped the result.

## Observable indicators

- Clarifying questions
- Restatements or inferred requirements
- Intermediate plans or representations
- Changes between output modes
- Decisions made without human confirmation
