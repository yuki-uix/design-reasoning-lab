# Decision Boundary

## Status

Working concept.

## Definition

The point at which responsibility for a design decision is assigned to the human, the AI system, or a negotiated interaction between them.

## Competing definitions

The boundary may be better modeled as dynamic ownership over time rather than a fixed division.

## Open questions
